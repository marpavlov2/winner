Pot of Gold

Pot of gold is a multiplayer mode where all players' bets are pooled together to form a Pot of gold. The winner of the Jackpot takes all

Here's how it works:

1

Place your bet. The higher your bet, the higher your chance of winning

2

Wait for the required number of transactions in the current round or place new bets

3

The winner will be selected once the required number of transactions is received

4

The winner takes all! The smart contract automatically transfers the Jackpot to the winner's wallet

Pot of TON

Pot of TON is a multiplayer mode where all players' bets are pooled together to form a Pot of TON. The winner of the round takes all. Award can grow to be very large, offering the potential for a big win. The smart contract ensures that the outcome of the game is random and fair.

Here's how it works:

Place your bet. The higher your bet, the higher your chance of winning

Wait for the required number of transactions in the current round or place new bets

The winner will be selected once the required number of transactions is received.

The smart contract automatically transfers the award to the winner's wallet
